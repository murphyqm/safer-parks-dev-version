Privacy & Cookies

Privacy Policy

The Safer Parks Dashboard is a research tool developed by the University of Leeds. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website.

Information We Collect

This dashboard is a static website with no user authentication or data collection systems. We do not collect personal information through forms or user interactions. Basic server logs may be generated by your web hosting provider for security and analytics purposes.

Data Processing

The geographic data displayed in this dashboard (park boundaries, park features, routes, etc.) is publicly available open spatial data. No personal data is processed by the dashboard itself. All analysis happens locally in your browser.

Third-Party Services

The dashboard uses the following third-party services:

  • Leaflet.js: An open-source mapping library
  • OpenStreetMap: Tile layer provider for map backgrounds
  • Pico CSS: CSS framework for styling

Please consult their respective privacy policies for more information.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. Your browser may store session data temporarily as part of normal web functionality, but this dashboard does not set any persistent cookies.

Contact & More Information

For questions about privacy, data protection, or the handling of personal data, please contact the University of Leeds in accordance with GDPR requirements.

For research-related inquiries about this dashboard, please visit our About page or complete the feedback form linked throughout the dashboard.
